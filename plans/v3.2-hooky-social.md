# v3.2 — The Social Loop + Battle Spectacle (response to founder playtest)

Verdicts from playtest: battles suck · believe (💛) does nothing visible · many dead buttons · no countdown · no scribbit detail view · no multiplayer hook · overall sucks. All addressed below.

## 1. THE MULTIPLAYER HOOK — "Back a Scribbit" (new core social loop)
Every day you get ONE Back (a bet) to place on any of tonight's rumble entrants — yours or anyone's.
- Rumble resolves → backers of the champion earn **Clout** (+3 win, +1 finalist). Clout is permanent, on the leaderboard and flair: the "talent scout" ladder.
- Why it's hooky: your bet forces a return visit; browsing today's entrants (gallery) is daily content made of player drawings; comments become handicapping ("moss chonker counters the forecast, easy money").
- Countdown everywhere: "⚔️ Rumble resolves in 3h 12m" (ArenaState.rumbleResolvesAt).

## 2. BATTLE SPECTACLE + PHASER INNOVATION (the $5K story)
- **Drawings come alive**: WebGL maps the player PNG onto Phaser 4.2 Mesh2D
  geometry (25 vertices, static optimized topology); Canvas uses the proven 3x3
  slice fallback. Idle breathing, birth unfolding, lunge stretch, impact ripple,
  and KO folding all deform the submitted pixels.
- **Motion DNA**: the dominant analyzed stat selects a named Shape Power —
  INKQUAKE, NIB HALO, SMEARSTEP, or COLORBURST — so drawing decisions control
  both combat numbers and visible motion.
- **Server-authored live spectacle**: a deterministic 20 Hz simulation resolves
  the winner and stores bounded sparse events plus motion checkpoints. Phaser
  interpolates the transcript as continuous wall-bouncing action, so replay is
  cheap, cheat-resistant, and does not require realtime infrastructure.
- Cinematic pass: camera shake on hits, slow motion on the final fold,
  element-particle move FX, and a TAP-TO-CHEER meter (cosmetic: confetti + louder
  announcer; interaction without desync).
- Defeat has a next step: loss card shows lifespan left + REMATCH (spar) + "Back someone tonight" CTA. No dead ends.

## 3. SCRIBBIT DETAIL MODAL
Tap any scribbit anywhere → sticker-card modal: big art, name/artist, level+XP bar, mood, stats 2x2, W/L, belief + backers, days-left pips, battle history (tap → replay), actions contextual: care/spar/enter (mine) · believe/back (others').

## 3.5 PERMANENT COLLECTION LOOP
- Daily Ink Trail makes the existing reward economy obvious: draw +2 · each care +1 · first spar win +2, with the first capsule each UTC day discounted to 5 Ink.
- Capsule discovery survives consumed accessory copies and fading Scribbits. Pens, titles, collector rank, and the album are permanent account progress.
- Show the exact collection count and pulls remaining until guaranteed Epic before spending. Gacha is earned-only and cosmetic; drawing remains combat authority.
