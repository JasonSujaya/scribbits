## What this app is

Scribbits Arena is a portrait Reddit game where each player draws one creature, called a Scribbit, and the drawing's shape becomes its combat build. The server analyzes submissions, owns progression and outcomes, and returns deterministic battle reports; the client presents those reports as lively real-time-looking replays. A Scribbit grows for three days, remains playable with locked base stats when Mature, and becomes a permanent Archived card when its owner retires it or the three-slot Mature roster overflows. (`app/src/shared/arena.ts`, `app/src/server/core/scribbit.ts`)

## Why it's valuable

The hook is immediate and personal: a player's own drawing visibly changes how a fight unfolds, but cannot buy raw stat power. The critical path is **draw a Scribbit → submit it → watch that exact drawing fight → understand the result**; if any link breaks, the game loses its reason to exist. (`app/src/client/scenes/Draw.ts`, `app/src/server/core/battle.ts`)

## The main flows

1. **A player opens the Reddit post → enters the expanded game → chooses the timed Community Theme or untimed Free Draw.** The Community Theme creates the daily Scribbit, brings it alive, immediately offers three distinct Power-Ups with weighted randomized rarities and shuffled card order, and requires one choice before that exact drawing enters its first fight against a simple server-selected founding NPC; the result then returns to Home. Free Draw uses the same canvas but saves separately without a Rumble entry, battle, or reward. Returning to Draw on the same Arena day shows that verified saved image and one Practice action. The server rechecks every PNG and owns both write paths. (`app/src/client/splash.ts`, `app/src/client/scenes/ScribbitHome.ts`, `app/src/client/scenes/Draw.ts`, `app/src/server/routes/api.ts`, `app/src/server/core/freeDrawingStore.ts`)
2. **The server resolves a role fight → the player watches a deterministic replay → the result returns to the right scene.** Brown/coral/orange drawings become close-range Brawlers, gold/green/blue drawings become long-range Longshots, and aqua/purple/pink drawings become ranged Mages; black/grey/white stay neutral. The visible counter loop is exact: Brawler beats Mage, Mage beats Longshot, and Longshot beats Brawler. Each role owns permanent visible weaponry, movement, basic attacks, and a signature move; Gear only adds bounded sidegrades. The fixed-tick engine owns outcomes and the arena micro-goal score, while Replay only projects the immutable timeline. (`app/src/shared/combat/roles.ts`, `app/src/shared/combat/engine.ts`, `app/src/client/lib/roleweaponrenderer.ts`, `app/src/client/scenes/Replay.ts`)
3. **A returning player opens Home → sees their living Scribbit → taps one large Draw button when they want to create or practice.** Home is the centered primary dock destination and intentionally does not show care, detail, or battle controls; Arena remains the fighting hub. (`app/src/client/scenes/ScribbitHome.ts`, `app/src/client/lib/appdock.ts`, `app/src/client/scenes/Draw.ts`)
4. **A returning player opens Arena → sees the active season, personal rank and points, current scoring event, and today's venue challenge before choosing how to compete.** The venue card keeps the player's best challenge progress, stamps a completed goal, previews the next venue unlock, and opens a separate daily fastest-clear ranking. The Arena then presents one selected Scribbit and two focused challenge cards: Season Rumble for the ranking Pick and the daily Champion Contract. (`app/src/client/scenes/ArenaHome.ts`, `app/src/client/lib/seasonboard.ts`, `app/src/client/lib/venueboard.ts`) The venue's rule, micro-goal, stamp, and ranking remain server-authored. (`app/src/shared/battlearena.ts`, `app/src/server/core/venueStamp.ts`) Arena is the one home for starting living-Scribbit competition; Battles remains replay history, Draw owns creation and role Practice, and the generic Scribbit detail modal owns care and inspection. (`app/src/client/scenes/ArenaHome.ts`)
5. **A player opens Arena → makes one Rumble Pick → the server locks that daily prediction → the nightly job awards Clout and files a receipt.** Rumble Pick is a compact secondary Arena action, not a fight mode; the client never decides eligibility, placement, or payout. (`app/src/client/scenes/ArenaHome.ts`, `app/src/server/core/clout.ts`, `app/src/server/core/dailyJob.ts`)
6. **A player finishes the first Rival Run → follows `FIRST GEAR` through only the Care still needed → opens a simplified first chest → lands in Bag on the reward's equipment category.** The server refreshes the exact Ink and Care state before each step, and the first chest always grants equippable Gear while preserving the published rarity odds. Later Shop visits support one or ten Mystery Ink Chests; ten is the largest batch, Epic pity lands by ten, duplicate copies remain Forge material, and the server persists every open and loadout. (`app/src/client/lib/firstchesttrail.ts`, `app/src/client/scenes/Replay.ts`, `app/src/client/scenes/ArenaHome.ts`, `app/src/client/scenes/Shop.ts`, `app/src/client/lib/capsulemachine.ts`, `app/src/server/core/inkStore.ts`)
7. **A player opens Gallery from Home → immediately sees owned Scribbits split into Growing, Mature, and Archived.** Growing and Mature each have three slots and can be deliberately retired from their detail card; the drawing and final record move into Archived. When a fourth Scribbit matures, the oldest Mature Scribbit is archived automatically. Archived cards are kept permanently and read four at a time in the current book layout. (`app/src/client/scenes/Gallery.ts`, `app/src/server/core/scribbit.ts`)

## Expected user behavior

Players draw in portrait from Home's large Draw button, then use Gallery as the immediate home for their Growing, Mature, and Archived Scribbits. Growing and Mature show explicit three-slot capacities and a confirmed Retire action; Archived is permanent and paginated rather than destructively capped. (`app/src/client/scenes/Gallery.ts`, `app/src/shared/legacycards.ts`)

Players should recognize a Scribbit as Brawler, Longshot, or Mage before reading its exact four stats, then understand who it beats from the explicit matchup label, permanent weapon silhouette, and range behavior. They watch server-authored fights, make one meaningful daily Pick from Arena, open Shop for earned-Ink Mystery Chests, and open Bag for equipment. (`app/src/shared/combat/roles.ts`, `app/src/client/scenes/Draw.ts`, `app/src/client/scenes/Replay.ts`)

## Not built yet

- There is no synchronous networked PvP transport; combat is server-resolved and then streamed from an immutable report as a replay. (`app/src/server/core/battle.ts`, `app/src/client/scenes/Replay.ts`)
- Earned Gear resolves into at most one bounded technique per category in Exhibition/Spar, with the strongest piece leading and the second supporting. Its stable Gear snapshot remains v1 inside deterministic combat transcript v4; it does not replace the permanent role weapon or change the fixed 100-point drawing build. Rumble, Champion, and Practice remain Gear-neutral by design. Birth-time accessories remain a separate consumed, baked-image system. (`app/src/shared/gearcombat.ts`, `app/src/shared/combat/types.ts`, `app/src/server/core/battle.ts`, `app/tests/gear-combat-balance.test.mjs`)
- Scout Notebook is no longer a primary navigation destination. Its scene and API remain temporarily for saved-replay compatibility and can be removed after those return paths no longer reference it. (`app/src/client/scenes/ScoutNotebook.ts`, `app/src/server/core/scoutNotebook.ts`)
- The disabled `Reddit Gold Styles` banner is only a coming-soon preview; no product, entitlement, paid randomization, or fulfillment route exists. (`app/src/client/lib/capsulemachine.ts`)
- Remaining cleanup opportunities are tracked in `SLOP-AUDIT.md`; none is a second gameplay authority path.
